Reality Motion
How REALITY moves

Stamped,
not floated.

Motion in REALITY behaves like the print it comes from: things stamp down with a little overshoot, lift when you touch them, and ink misregisters into place. Nothing drifts or fades aimlessly. This is the spec — the curves, the durations, and what triggers them. The full library of named animations lives, playable, in the Design System page.

01

Four rules

01
Stamp, don't float

Entrances punch in with a slight overshoot and settle — like a stamp hitting paper. No slow fades from nowhere.

02
Touch lifts

Interactive things rise a few px and the shadow deepens on hover; they press back down on click. The shadow is the feedback.

03
Misregister into place

Accent layers slide in offset and converge — the riso print that didn't quite register. Reserved for hero moments.

04
Move with purpose

Every motion has a trigger and an end-state. If it can't be justified, it doesn't move. Decorative loops stay out of content.

02

Easing curves

Three curves cover everything. Watch the dot — same distance, different feel.
Stamp
--ease-stamp
cubic-bezier(0.2, 1.4, 0.45, 1)

Overshoot punch. Entrances, counters landing, badges, anything that should feel stamped. The 1.4 control point pushes past the target then snaps back.

Snap
--ease-snap
cubic-bezier(0.3, 0, 0.2, 1)

Quick settle. Buttons, toggles, hovers, presses — short state changes that need to feel immediate and crisp, no overshoot.

Glide
--ease-out
cubic-bezier(0.16, 1, 0.3, 1)

Long decel. Number rolls, kinetic headlines, large entrances and surface crossfades — a fast start that eases out over distance.

03

Duration scale

Four steps. Bars play at true speed — short means short.
Tap--dur-tap
120ms
Hover lift & button press. Instant tactile feedback, paired with Snap.
Quick--dur-quick
200ms
Chips, small toggles, selection states. Snappy but visible.
Settle--dur-settle
350ms
Day/Night flip and surface crossfades. The whole page eases between palettes.
Entrance--dur-enter
700ms
Counters, kinetic headlines, hero reveals. Siblings stagger by ~90ms so a group ripples in.

06

Reduced motion

The contract

When a visitor sets prefers-reduced-motion: reduce, REALITY honours it system-wide: all transitions are switched off, auto-loops never start, and any entrance shows its end-state immediately instead of animating in. Nothing important is ever gated behind a motion the visitor has opted out of — the page is fully usable, just still.
