Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to know people to participate in events?

No. Unless marked as private, all our events welcome newcomers. Come alone and meet people, or bring friends - either works.

Can I work on my laptop here?

Yes, until 5pm daily. After that, laptops away as we transition from workspace to social space.

Is smoking allowed?

Outdoors only. Enjoy the view from our third-floor balcony.

What's your code of conduct?

Respect everyone. No discrimination. We want this to be a comfortable space for all.

I'd like to host an event at Reality. How does that work?

Talk to a member of management about your idea. If it fits our space and community, we'll help you make it happen.
